Study and Success Resources

At the University of Michigan, academic success isn’t just about attending lectures—it’s about finding the right resources to support your learning journey. Whether you need tutoring, advising, study spaces, or academic planning, this page will help you navigate essential resources available to UMSI students.

Why Academic Support Matters

  • Stay on track with advising - Meet with academic advisors to plan your courses and ensure you're meeting graduation requirements.
  • Tutoring can make a difference - Whether you're struggling with a subject or just want to reinforce your learning, tutoring services are available.
  • Find the perfect study environment - Discover the best spots on campus for focused or collaborative study sessions

What You'll Find on This Page

  • Academic Advising: Learn how to schedule advising sessions and access degree-planning resources.
  • Tutoring Services: Find academic help for difficult subjects, writing support, and peer-led study groups.
  • Study Spaces: Explore the best study locations across campus, from quiet libraries to group-friendly areas.
  • Additional Academic Resources: Access time management tools, research help, and academic workshops.
  • Need Help Right Now?

    If you have academic concerns, schedule an appointment with an advisor through the BSI Advising Resource Site.

Academic Advising: Planning Your Path

What is Academic Advising?

Academic advisors help you with course selection, degree planning, career guidance, and navigating challenges.

UMSI Academic Advising Resources

  • BSI Advising Resource Site – BSI Advising
  • How to Make an Appointment – Advising is available via in-person, virtual, or email consultations. Check the UMSI Student Affairs Office for scheduling.
  • Workshops and Info Sessions – Stay updated on advising events through UMSI's student portal.

Pro Tip: Meet with an advisor at least once a semester to stay on track with your academic goals and career goals!

Tutoring: Get Help When You Need It

Why Tutoring?

Tutoring is a great way to strengthen your understanding of difficult subjects, get personalized support, and boost your confidence.

Tutoring Resources at U-M

  • UM Tutoring Services: Tutoring at U-M - Find subject-specific tutoring, writing help, and peer-led sessions.
  • SI-Specific Tutoring: Some UMSI courses offer peer tutoring- check with your professor or academic advisor for options.
  • Group Study Sessions: Join or create study groups through student organizations or learning centers.

Pro Tip: Don't wait until exams- utilize tutoring early in the semester for the best results!

Best Study Locations at U-M

Different students need different study environments—some prefer silence, while others thrive in collaborative spaces.

Quiet & Focused

  • Hatcher Graduate Library - Quiet floors for deep focus.
  • Law Library - Aesthetic, silent, and great for solo studying.

Group and Collaborative

  • Shapiro (UGLi) Library - Whiteboards, group tables, and study rooms.
  • Duderstadt Center ("The Dude") - Engineering, tech-focused, and group-friendly.

Cozy & Relaxed

  • Literati Coffee - A student favorite for casual study sessions.
  • Munger Graduate Residences - Grad student-exclusive spaces for quiet work

Pro Tip: Book study rooms in advance, especially during finals week!

Additional Academic Resources

  • Writing Support: Sweetland Center for Writing - Get help with essays, research papers, and resumes.
  • Library Research Help: UM Library Services - Access research tools, citation help, and librarian consultations.
